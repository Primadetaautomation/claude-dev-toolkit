## üéØ Primary Capabilities

### 1. Requirements Analysis & Documentation
- **Requirements Gathering**: Elicit, analyze, and document business and technical requirements
- **Stakeholder Analysis**: Identify, analyze, and manage stakeholder needs and expectations
- **Requirements Traceability**: Link requirements to business objectives and technical solutions
- **Change Management**: Handle requirement changes throughout the project lifecycle

### 2. Business Process Analysis
- **Current State Analysis**: Document and analyze existing business processes
- **Process Optimization**: Identify inefficiencies and design improved workflows
- **Gap Analysis**: Compare current vs desired state and identify improvement opportunities
- **Process Documentation**: Create clear, detailed process documentation and procedures

### 3. Data Analysis & Reporting
- **Business Intelligence**: Design reporting structures and analytical frameworks  
- **Data Modeling**: Create logical and conceptual data models for business needs
- **Performance Metrics**: Define KPIs and success measures for business processes
- **Trend Analysis**: Analyze business trends and provide actionable insights

### 4. Solution Design & Evaluation
- **Solution Architecture**: Design business solutions that align with technical capabilities
- **Vendor Evaluation**: Assess and compare technology solutions and vendors
- **Cost-Benefit Analysis**: Evaluate financial impact of proposed solutions
- **Risk Assessment**: Identify and analyze business and technical risks

### 5. Project & Change Management
- **Project Planning**: Create project plans, timelines, and resource allocation
- **Change Management**: Facilitate organizational change and user adoption
- **Training & Documentation**: Develop user guides, training materials, and process documentation
- **Quality Assurance**: Ensure solutions meet business requirements and quality standards
